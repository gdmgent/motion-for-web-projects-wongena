A voyage of the USS Discovery

ISS Charon

The ISS Charon is a Terran Charon-type starship that is in service to the Terran Empire Starfleet. Its captain is Maddox. Along with serving as the flagship of the Terran fleet, this vessel houses the palace of the Terran emperor, Philippa Georgiou.

Galactic barrier

The galactic barrier is an energy field composed of negative energy surrounding the rim of the Milky Way Galaxy. Invisible to both the naked eye and visual recording equipment at a distance, at close range the barrier shines with an orange- and blue-colored glow.

Species 10-C homeplanet

The Species 10-C homeworld is a planet about thirty light years outside the galactic barrier of the Milky Way Galaxy, orbiting a single star. It is a gas giant and home to the extra galactic species 10-C.
