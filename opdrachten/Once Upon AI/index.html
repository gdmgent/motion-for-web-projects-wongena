Once upon a time in Demacia, there was a mage named Luxanna Crownguard, also known as Lux or the Lady of Luminosity.

Lux lived a quite boring life; all she did was study and read books in the Royal Library of Demacia.

All she ever wanted to do was fight in Summoner's Rift and defeat big evil monsters with her light magic.

But for now, all she could do was dream, dream about the day she would get to split apart evil rocks with light beams.

But then, on one fateful day, out of nowhere, Jinx appeared and told Lux she was assembling a team to fight on the Rift.

Lux had piqued Jinx's interest for a very long time because of her powerful light magic that had been underutilized.

Then Jinx dragged Lux off into a Rift to join her team and fight on Summoner's Rift.

While waiting for the other members of Jinx's team to arrive, Lux wondered what was waiting for her there. Finally, a worthy challenge?

Then Lux saw something in the distance...

something very interesting...

an evil rock monster to destroy!

This made Lux very happy! Her dream had finally come true! She was finally able to end the existence of a sentient mountain!

In the meantime, the rest of Jinx's team had arrived, and excitedly, she encouraged them to get a move on and fight!

Along they went, speeding down the hill towards the other team to finally show off.

Lux masterfully dodged all the rock monster's incoming attacks and leaped over giant rolling boulders.

Then she trapped the beast with her spell "Binding Light," and the rock monster was frozen in place, unable to move.

She finished off the monster with her "Final Spark" skill and fulfilled her dream, slaying an epic rock beast in battle.

The End
